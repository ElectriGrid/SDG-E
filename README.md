# San Diego Gas & Electric (SDG&E) Exploration
SDG&E is one of the three major investor-owned utilities (IOUs) in California. As part of the capstone deliverable, limited distributed energy resources (DER) distribution are calculated and mapped. These limited DER results are then correlated with socio-demographic characteristics to assess environmental justice concerns. 

SDG&E hosts its data in a different format than the other IOUs in California (PG&E and SCE). Notably, the downloaded data is in polygons while other IOUs hold their feederlines as lines. This repository explores the format that SDG&E holds its data and troubleshoots first steps to standardizing SDG&E data to the format of the other IOUs.
